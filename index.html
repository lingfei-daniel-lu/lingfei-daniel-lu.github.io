About me

Welcome to my website! I am currently a Ph.D candidate in Economics at HKUST Business School.

I received the B.Sc. in Economics and Finance from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, in 2020, and the M.Phil. in Economics from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, in 2022.

My main research fields are international trade and international finance. My interest is in studying the firm behaviors under global shocks. My current research works focus on topics about (1) exchange rate pass-through; (2) credit constraints; and (3) monetary policy transmission.
